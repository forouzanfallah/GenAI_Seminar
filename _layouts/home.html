CSE 598: Frontier Topics in GenAI Seminar (Fall 24)


Arizona State University

Overview

In the ever-evolving landscape of Generative AI, we witness exponential growth, notably in topics like large-language models, text-to-image, video generation, multimodal reasoning, drug discovery, robotics, and beyond. Applied research thrives, yet it is imperative to spotlight foundational works. Introducing the "Frontier Topics in Generative AI" Seminar Series. This series focuses on an in-depth exploration of grounded research in GenAI, including vision, language, and other pivotal areas. Join us in unraveling the core principles propelling innovation, shaping the future of this dynamic field.
