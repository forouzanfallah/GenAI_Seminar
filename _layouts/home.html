CSE 598: Frontier Topics in GenAI Seminar (Fall 24)


Arizona State University

Overview

Join our "Frontier Topics in Generative AI" Seminar Series as the field experiences unprecedented growth. Delving into areas such as large-language models, text-to-image synthesis, video generation, multimodal reasoning, science discovery, and robotics becomes increasingly vital. While applied research flourishes, it remains crucial to illuminate the foundational works underpinning these advancements. Our seminars offer an in-depth exploration of GenAI, covering key domains like vision and language, and unraveling the core principles that drive innovation and shape the future of this dynamic field. These sessions are part of course CSE 598 at Arizona State University, scheduled on Tuesdays and Thursdays from 9:00 AM to 10:15 AM. Dates for each talk are listed alongside the corresponding speaker's name.
